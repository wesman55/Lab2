What We Provide

Sustainable Gardens provides a variety of services in both landscaping and irrigation. We specialize in edible gardening though our expertise extends to all areas of landscaping.

Sustainable Gardens has been serving the Phoenix Metropolitan Area for 15 years and we have a large variety of services listed below. Contact Us if you cannot find the service you are looking for below.

All services listed below include a free preliminary consulation where we will extensively describe all aspects of an installation or mantainance plan.

To begin the process, after selecting a desired service call (602) 534-5678 or email us at sustainablegardens.az@gmail.com to set up an appointment.

Appointments must be made 24 hours in advance.
